Latent variable models (LVMs) are well-established statistical models where some of the variables are not observed. lvm4j implements popular LVMs in the Java programming language.

Take me to Github.

Documentation

Find the lvm4j -API at javadoc.io and the tutorial here.

Installation

Just put this block into your pom.xml and maven does the rest. 

  <dependency>
    <groupId>net.digital-alexandria</groupId>
    <artifactId>lvm4j</artifactId>
    <version>0.2</version>
  </dependency>
